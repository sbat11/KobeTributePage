Kobe "Bean" Bryant

One of the Greatest to Ever Do it

Here is a timeline of all of Kobe's biggest moments:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

  • 1984 - Moved to Italy with his family as his dad played professional basketball there

  • 1991 - Came back to the USA and started playing basketball

  • 1995 - Earned the senior MVP award at the Adidas ABCD Camp

  • 1996 - Decided to declare the for the NBA draft and was drafted by the Charlotte Hornets and then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers

  • 1997 - Became the youngest slam dunk contest champion at the age of 18

  • 1998 - Became the youngest NBA All-star started in history

  • 1999 - After Phil Jackson took over, he became one of the best shooting guards in the league

  • Mid-1999 - Won the Lakrs an NBA championship along side Shaquille O'Neal

  • 2000 - Beat Allen Iverson and the Philadelphia 76ers to win his 2nd championship

  • 2001 - Beat the New Jersey Nets in the NBA Finals which led to his reputation as a "clutch player"

  • 2004 - Lost to the "Bad Boy Pistons" in the NBA finals

  • 2006 - Scored a career-high 81 points against the Raptors having the second-highest point total in history

  • 2006 - Changed his number to 24 at the beginning of the 2006-07 season

  • 2008 - Won USA a gold medal in the 2008 Olympics

  • 2006 - Made six game-winning shots that year and became the youngest to reach 25,000 points

  • Mid-2010 - Beat the Boston Celtics for his 5th championship

  • 2016 - Played his final game and scored 60 points against the Utah Jazz

  • 2020 - Died at the age of 41

"I have nothing in common with lazy people who blame others for their lack of success. Great things come from hard work and perseverance. No excuses."

--Kobe Bryant
